Owari is the alias of Oliver Still, an artist, producer, and engineer from Tunbridge Wells, Kent. Even in his early stages, Owari’s work is already showing a unique impression on the indie electronic genre. Taking inspiration from the ambience of everyday life, Owari's sound develops its own honest personality with each production, offering a soft and thoughtful place for the listener to connect. This ability to connect is what drives Owari in his expedition for authenticity - a place many of us wish to be.

Following his early features on BBC Music Introducing and late-night BBC Radio 1, Owari was asked to officially remix MJ Cole’s single ‘Pictures In My Head’ with his debut single selected to be apart of Slow Magic’s mixtape for Apple Music. Recently, singles from the Imperfectionist EP have accumulated over 60,000 streams and have featured on Spotify’s Selectronica editorial playlist. Additionally, the EP has been championed and supported by Charlie Bones from NTS Radio, Laurence Guy on Rinse FM, and by BBC Music Introducing. Owari’s influences stem from artists such as Maribou State, Indian Wells, and Gold Panda.

Owari now hosts a monthly residency on Balamii Radio, presenting a seamless exploration into the ambient and left-field music that inspires his wellbeing and the project.
